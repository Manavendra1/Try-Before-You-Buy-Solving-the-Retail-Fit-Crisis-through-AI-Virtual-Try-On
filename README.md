"Try Before You Buy": AI Virtual Try-On Ecosystem
Welcome to the Group 6 Design Thinking Initiative repository! We are on a mission to transform the retail experience by solving the Retail Fit Crisis through our innovative AI Virtual Try-On solution. 


he Retail Fit Crisis & The Emotional Dimension
Online apparel shopping currently faces a massive challenge: a 30% average return rate that costs retailers $60 billion annually. The root cause is fit and size uncertainty because shoppers cannot physically try on clothes before purchasing. Without the tactile experience of a fitting room, customers order multiple sizes, leading to wasteful returns and frustrated experiences. 


Traditional analytics often miss the deeper story, but our ethnographic interviews revealed the emotional dimension of returns: hesitation at checkout, guilt over keeping items, and distrust in sizing accuracy. Through a human-centered approach, we completely reframed the problem. Instead of relying on reactive logistics to process returns faster and cheaper, we focus on proactive design to eliminate fit uncertainty and build absolute user trust. 


The Solution: A Phygital Try-On Ecosystem
Our core innovation is the Phygital Try-On Ecosystem. This revolutionary approach seamlessly bridges digital convenience with physical precision. At its heart is a decentralized, privacy-first 3D digital avatar that empowers users across both online and local boutique environments.  One avatar. Two worlds. Zero compromise on privacy or precision. 


Essential Brainstormed Features

Mobile 3D Body Scanner: Capture a full body scan in under 1 minute using only a smartphone. 

In-Store Endless Aisle Kiosks: Interactive digital try-on stations tailored for boutiques. 

Zero-Retention Data Vault: Ensures complete privacy through local rendering with no photo storage. 

Fit Heatmaps: A visual display that highlights garment tightness, looseness, and tension points. 
+1

Universal Sizing Passport: A unified cross-retailer sizing profile. 

Shared Virtual Cart: Introduces social shopping by allowing friend feedback. 

The User Journey
Capture: Using just a smartphone and no special equipment, the user takes a few photos or a short video in under 60 seconds. 

Generate: AI photogrammetry instantly creates a personal 3D digital twin featuring 80+ precise body measurements, rendered locally with zero data retention for complete privacy. 

Browse, Assess & Purchase: Users visualize clothing on their avatar utilizing fit heatmaps, allowing them to make confident purchase decisions without sizing guesswork. 

Meeting Diverse User Needs
Online Shopper & Boutique Customer: Seeks sizing confidence to avoid wasteful returns and wants a reliable try-on experience without the hassle of shipping items back. 

Boutique Owner: Needs to reduce operational costs, minimize unsold inventory, and enhance the customer experience. 

Tech-Savvy & Privacy-Conscious Users: Demands secure, private data handling with full control over personal information and transparency in AI recommendations. 

Idea Validation & Business Impact
Our research heavily validates the AI Virtual Try-On solution. 

Trust & Tech Viability: 70% of surveyed users trust AI fit recommendations. Our technology achieves 96-97% measurement accuracy in under 45 seconds, a metric validated by platforms like 3DLOOK and TrueToForm. 


Business & Environmental Impact: AI-powered virtual try-ons are proven to reduce return rates by 40%, actively cutting into the $60B annual return cost. Furthermore, this solution lowers the environmental footprint by minimizing packaging waste and returns.
